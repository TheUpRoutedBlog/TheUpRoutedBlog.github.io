OUR ROUTES

Welcome to UpRouted, our blog dedicated to all things travel! Here, you can read about our adventures, laugh at our disasters and learn from both our triumphs and misfortunes. We are two money-conscious backpackers (and a not-so-money-conscious mouse) desperate to explore as much of the world as our pockets will allow. We hope you enjoy reading about our experiences as much as we enjoy writing about them. So, who exactly are we?

Name: Brianna Mills
Never leave England without: Swiss-army knife, flip-flops, a head torch and of course Cheddar.
Country would most like to go back to: Colombia.
What I like to do when I’m not travelling: Being a teacher while pretending to speak Spanish and drawing all over maps.
Top of my current wish list: Stumble across a tiger in Bangladesh.


Name: Alexander Booth
Best burger ever eaten: The Ruben-topped burger at the Grand Trunk Pub, Detroit, USA.
Favourite country visited so far: Peru.
What I like to do when I’m not travelling: Physics things, playing squash and looking up the latest flight deals.
Top of my current wish list: Drive a 4x4 along the Pamir highway.


Name: Cheddar Mills
Favourite culinary destination: Paris of course, all the scrumptious cheese, pate and wine you can eat.
Smelliest travel destination: Antartica - penguins could learn a thing or two about personal grooming from us mice!
What I like to do when I’m not travelling: Wining and dining with my more sophisticated friends and keeping Brianna from making a fool of herself (well when I can). Basically enjoying the finer things in life that my travelling compadres don't care for as much!
