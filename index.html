Welcome to UpRouted, our blog dedicated to all things travel! Here, you can read about our experiences, laugh at our disasters and learn from both our triumphs and misfortunes. We are two money-conscious backpackers (and a not-so-money-conscious mouse) desperate to explore as much of the world as our pockets will allow. Our latest adventure is an overland journey from our home in the UK, to the shores of Australia. We hope you enjoy reading about our experiences as much as we enjoy writing about them. Our coding skills do not yet stretch to optimising the site for small screens, such as iPhones. Bear with us, for now stick to a larger screen where possible!

The leg of pretty cities, fine wine and not one airline

By Brianna   28/Sep/16    Comments soon!

One month and 10 countries later, Burgas, Bulgaria was destined to be the final stop on the 'Europe' leg of our overland venture. It had been a rapid gallop across our home continent, leaving us no time to process how far we'd come, and the enormous distances we still had to cover over the next few months. With Turkey and The Caucasus waiting for us in the East, we had a little time to reflect on all that had happened...

The realm of full moons, Transylvania and dictator-mania

By Brianna   22/Sep/16    Comments soon!

Hello friends! According to the full moon I can see from our train to Baku, Azerbaijan, its been a whole lunar cycle since we travelled through Romania. With mountains, mystery and a darkly fascinating history, this country had far more to offer than we could cram into our tight schedule. But that didn't mean we couldn't try to do it justice.

The country of goodwill, illegal entry dates and breakaway states

By Brianna   12/Sep/16    Comments soon!

Where is Moldova? Consistently the winner of 'the least visited country in Europe' award, it would seem the most popular answer is 'Who cares?'. In truth, its fun-loving, good-natured culture and its intriguing, politically complex separatist region were reason enough to make it an unmissable stop on our whirlwind European tour.

The land of mix-ups, extra miles & stone faced smiles

By Brianna   31/Aug/16    Comments soon!

Welcome to Ukraine, where the train stations are grander than your average European palace and underpasses are consumer havens. Where cultural misunderstandings lead to a great time and expecting the unexpected is all part of the fun. As long as you don't smile while you're having all this fun. After all, this is the land where smiling is reserved for con-men and fools.
