Welcome to UpRouted, our blog dedicated to all things travel! Here, you can read about our experiences, laugh at our disasters and learn from both our triumphs and misfortunes. We are two money-conscious backpackers (and a not-so-money-conscious mouse) desperate to explore as much of the world as our pockets will allow. Our latest adventure is an overland journey from our home in the UK, to the shores of Australia. We hope you enjoy reading about our experiences as much as we enjoy writing about them.
